“Electronics and Communication Engineering deals with the electronic devices and software applications. It is an interface of chip level hardware and information technology”.

Electronics and Communication Engineering is the branch that deals with designing and testing circuits that use the electromagnetic properties of electrical components such as resistors, capacitors, inductors and transistors to achieve a particular functionality. The Electronics & Communication Engineering Department of Cooch Behar Government Engineering College committed for providing highest possible standards of quality in teaching and practical. At present the department offers B.Tech. in Electronics & Communication Engineering. The department has established all new laboratory with state-of-art facilities with a view to nurture students in experimental set up.
